Lasagna

This is a recipe of a great Lasagna. This lasagna will make your kids super happy, and your friends in awe of your skills.

The recipe

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable broth, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons white wine
